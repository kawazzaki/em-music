Itroduction

A&M Music Club was officially created in 2012, under the slogan: "be bold, be heard" has more than 80 members and is the music center of ENSAM. The club is divided into two parts: the event, through the flagship events: THE Casting, Karaoke Party and LIVE SHOW, and the internal, which consists of daily rehearsals to improve the level of the members as well as their cohesion. The objective of the club is to promote music as a science and to represent the school during various musical events. The events aim to offer a respite to students and to break the routine of the rather tight system at ENSAM.
